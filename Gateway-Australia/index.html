Welcome to Sydney

Overview

Sydney, is the capital of New South Wales and one of Australia's largest cities.It is best known for its harbourfront Sydney Opera House, with a distinctive sail-like design. Sydney is home to over 5,000,000 residents. Sydney Tower's outdoor platform, the Skywalk, offers 360-degree views of the city and suburbs.
